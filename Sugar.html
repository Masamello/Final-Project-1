How to play

[Put Sugar A Lot]
This game is quite simple to play! All you have to use is only a mouth! Click the Spoon and put sugar into Pot as much as you can!
You can play this game to click the title.

Play well, you'll be a good player

[Tips]

The important thing is angle of spoon!
Adjust the angle well and drag it down quickly!
Then you will get a lot of sugar.

[Tips]

If you succeed to get a lot of sugar,
you will get situation like this image.
Then throw it carefully and boldly into the pot!
